📚 What is Carbon Footprint?

A carbon footprint is the total greenhouse gases produced by daily activities such as transportation, electricity use, flights, and food consumption.

🌱 How to Reduce It?

  • Use public transport
  • Reduce electricity wastage
  • Choose sustainable food options
  • Plant trees
  • Avoid unnecessary flights
